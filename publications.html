A Warning from the Yakima: Washington's Water Future in a Warming Climate

Three data visualizations — a reservoir map, 130 years of storage records, and a Palmer Drought Severity Index chart — make the case that the Yakima Basin's water crisis is not a temporary dry spell but a fundamental, climate-driven shift in regional water availability.
